英语连词的简单总结

转折

用途: 前段与后段意思不同,或是有意义上的转折. 常用于表明相反或是相对的意思.

连接词意思用法例句
Nevertheless尽管如此句首/句中She was hungry, nevertheless, she gave her bread to a beggar.
On the contrary恰恰相反句中I did not go to London, on the contrary I went to Paris.
However然而句首/句中We thought he became a better person; however, he is not.
But然而/但是句首/句中The food is more expensive but its nutritional value is higher.
Although虽然句首/句中I will not buy the food although its nutritional value is higher.
in spite of尽管/虽然
Despite尽管
even though虽然
otherwise除此之外

Although 和 But 不能同时使用.

先后

用途: 散布于整篇文章中, 也可用于阐述论点. 使文章结构明确.

连接词意思用法例句
First, ... Second, ...第一…第二…段首/论点的句首There are two principles. First, the Principle of Equal Liberty, … . Second, the Difference Principle, … .
On one hand, … On the other hand, …一方面…另一方面…2个论点的句首On one hand, it exploits the income of the poor, … . On the other hand, it causes alienation, … .
simultaneously同时地句首
meanwhile当时/同时地

递进

用途: 用于说明两者以上的事物,且都存在共同点. 通常按照某种顺序排列, 表达程度呈现递增/递减的趋势.

连接词意思用法例句
Moreover, …此外/而且句首/句中I can cook myself now. Moreover, I can feel romance now.
Besides,此外/除…之外句首/句中The car is too expensive, and besides, it is not compulsory.
In addition,此外句首He is a professor. In addition, he is a television commentator.
then, …另外句首/句中She is good, but then so is he.
Furthermore, …而且/此外/再者句首/句中Furthermore, he is no longer hungry now.

并列

用途: 说明两者以上的事物,都有共同点,但是没有次序.

连接词意思用法例句
And和/以及句中Peter and Mary are my friends.
Either, … or … .要么…要么…常表达二选一It’s either black or grey. I can’t remember.
Both … and … .…和…Both Peter and Mary are my friends.
…as well as…也/以及句中When they go to Austria, they like walking as well as skiing.

强调

用途: 强调部分内容

连接词意思用法例句
Obviously明显地句首/句中Obviously, we can’t achieve all of these goals immediately.
Apparently显然地句首/句中He was apparently much surprised at the news.
In fact,事实上句首In fact, no one knows how the pyramids were built.
Undoubtedly,毫无疑问地句首/句中Undoubtedly, that is a sad news for us.

因果

用途: 描述因果关系, 推理逻辑关系.

连接词意思用法例句
due to由于句中/句首The accident was due to careless driving.
Hence,因此句首/句中She is obese and hence she need to keep fit.
Because因为句首/句中Don’t do it just because you can.
Therefore,所以句首/句中‘I think, therefore I am.’
as a result因此
thus从而/因而

比较

用途: 说明两者间的共同点, 指出一致的地方,其中一个是主体,一个是比较对象, 以比较对象来凸显主体的特别.

连接词意思用法例句
Equally同样地句首/句中/句尾Everyone should have human rights equally.
like像…一样句首/句中She spoke like an aristocrat.
similarly相似地句首/句中My brother was similarly threatened.
in comparison with与…相比句首/句中He is quite tall in comparison with his classmates.

对比

用途: 说明两者间的不同.

连接词意思用法例句
While尽管句首/句中Hong Kong is cold, while in Harbin it is even colder
Unlike与…相反句首/句中Mary and Tom are quite unlike each other
By contrast,相比之下句首By contrast, he is more handsome.
Nevertheless尽管如此句首/句中/句尾He is not smart, but I like him nevertheless.

总结

连接词意思用法例句
To conclude,作总结句首To conclude, the dinner is delicious.
To sum up,总而言之句首To sum up, Albert Camus is a great writer.
In summary,做归纳句首In summary, glucose is for respiration.
In conclusion作总结句首

举例

连接词意思用法例句
Such as,诸如此类句首/句中/句尾There are many things on the desk. Such as books, pens and computers.
For instance,例如句首/句中There are jobs more dangerous than truck driving; for instance training lions.
For example,例如句首/句中Many philosophers are great. For example, Nietzsche and Kant.
