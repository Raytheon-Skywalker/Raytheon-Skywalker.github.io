加拿大房屋信息表(房屋信息表（MLS Listing）详解)

租房买房都离不开MLS, 此处就尝试解释下MLS中的各种术语

Focus术语含义备注
A/CAir Conditioner, also CAC – Central Air Conditioner 空调 CAC是中央空调
AccAccess (as in Acc to backyard) 进入到… 如： 直接通往后院
AdAdvertisement 广告
Agragricultural (as in zoned Agr) 农业的
AgmtAgreement (as in Agmt of Purchase and Sale) 合约
A.K.A.“Also Known As” – as in, semi-detached a.k.a. “semi“. 另外被大家所熟知的名称
AlumAluminum (as in alum siding) 铝制的
AptApartment (as in Basement Apt) 公寓
ApxApproximate, also Appx 大约
Apx agethe age of the building
Apx sqftthe size of the unit in sqft- usually a range
Assessmentthis will likely be blank, but if not, it refers to the value the city has in it’s records for tax purposes评估：这可能是空白的，但如果没有，则指的是城市在其税收记录中的价值
BbqBarbeque 烧烤
B/IBuilt-In (as in B/I Dishwasher) 装修时包在里面的，内置的
B/I/DWBuilt-In Dishwasher 内置/嵌入式洗碗机
BerberA high quality broadloom/carpet 高级地毯
BkfstBreakfast (as in Bkfst Nook or Bkfst Room) 早餐
BldgBuilding
YesBldg Amenwill list some (but usually not all) of the amenities in the building将列出建筑物中的一些（但通常不是全部）设施
YesBldg Ins Inclis insurance for the building included in the maintenance fee ? (usually yes, but this of course would not cover your own condo contents)维修费中包含的建筑物的保险费是多少？ （通常是的，但这当然不会涵盖你自己的公寓内容）
YesBalconythis refers to a private balcony or terrace, not a common space
BRBedroom (as in 4BR) also Bdr 卧室，+1通常表示Den(书房)
BrdlmBroadloam (carpet) 地毯
BsbrdsBaseboards 踢脚线
BsmtBasement (as in finished Bsmt) 地下室
YesCable TV inclis cable tv included in the maintenance fee?
CACCentral Air Conditioning also C/A 中央空调
YesCAC Inclis central air conditioning hydro included in the maintenance fee?
Cert level
Closing day过户日。在这一天，你将正式开始拥有该物业。地产产权过户的日期，有时也称为closing date。在这一天，买卖双方一手交钱一手交货，即付款和地产过户都在这一天发生。律师和公证人的责任之一就是确保付款和过户，保障双方的利益。
Closing costs除了房子购买价格外的额外费用，如律师费和过户费等。这个费用要在过户当天支付，约为售价的1.5％到4%.
CombCombined (as in Comb Living Room/Dining Room or Comb LR/DR) 合在一起的
YesCommon Elem Inclare the common elements included in the maintenance fee? (it’s always Y for yes)
CondCondition or Conditional 条件，有条件的
Conditional offer有条件购买要约。例如，买主申请到按揭才生效的要约。这种有条件购买要约通常有时间限制。
CondoCondominium 高级公寓
Condo Fees跟Maint一样，维护费用
Counter offer在卖家给了报价要约后，屋主如果不接受报价可以提出还价，最权威的还价词汇就是 counteroffer。
CovCoverings (as in Window Cov – drapes,curtains, shears, etc) 窗帘
CvacCentral Vaccum (system) 中央吸尘器
DesigDesignation (as in Desig heritage property) 遗产房里会提到这个词
DetDetached (as in Det Garage or Det House or Semi Det) 独立屋
DirDirection 方向
DftDeal Fell Through (status condition on MLS) 交易告吹
YesDOMDays On Market (time listed as shown on MLS) 上市天数通常在多伦多这么火热的市场中，如果一套房子存在的时间超过了26天，就可以反映出这套房子可能在某一方面存在一些问题了。
DRDining Room 饭厅
DRsDoors 门
DryrDryer (as in Cloths Dryr) 干衣机
EACElectronic Air Cleaner 空气净化器
EgdoExtra Garage Door Opener 多的车房门开关
YesElev/Liftdoes the building have an elevator?
ELFsElectrical Lighting Fixtures 灯具
Energy Certrefers to whether the building has any energy efficiency certifications能源证书：指建筑物是否具有任何能效认证
YesEns Lndrydoes the unit have ensuite laundry?这个单位有洗衣房吗？房间内有洗衣机
EntEntrance (as in seperate Ent or Sep Ent)入口
ExclExcluded (as in Excl Chandelier)不包含在内
ExpExpired (as in Exp Listing, a status condition on MLS)过期
YesExposurethis refers to whether the unit is facing east, west, north or south(这是指该单位是面向东，西，北或南)
ExhExhaust (as in Exh fan or Exh to outside)排气扇
ExteriorIndicates the material makeup of the property’s exterior.外观材质
FAGForced Air Gas (as in FAG furnace or heating system) 燃气的热风取暖
FamFamily (as in Fam Room or Fam Rm, or FR or F/R 起居室起居室属于私领域，较注重空间的隐私感，位置通常在楼上或是较内部的区域，并常与私人卧室连结。
FeatFeature or Features (as in Feat a pool)
Foyer门厅
FinFinished 装修了的
FlFloor ,also Flrs (as in main Fl or hardwood Fl)楼层
Furnished
GarGarage, also Grg车库
YesGar/Gar SpcsIndicates if there is a garage with the property and the number of vehicles it can hold.表示是否有车库及其可容纳的车辆数量。
GdoGarage Door Opener车房开关
GB&EGas Burner & Equipment (furnace)燃气炉
Green PIS
YesHeatwhat kind of heat is in the building?
YesHeat inclis heat included in the maintenance fee?
Hi-EffHigh-Efficiency (furnace)高效的
Hwt(r)Hot Water Tank (rental)热水炉
YesHydro Inclis hydro(水电) included in the maintenance fee?
I/GInground (as in I/G pool)地平面以下的
InclIncluded包含在内
IntInterior or Interest (rate)内饰 或 利息
IrrIrregular, also Irreg (as in Irr lot size)不标准的
KitKitchen厨房
L/AListing Agent also L.A. 卖家经纪
LBOListing Broker Office (as in keys or documents at Lbo) 卖家经纪
Lndy Levwhat floor is the laundry located on?洗衣房在哪个楼层？
LocLocation 地点
Locker储物柜：指示是否有与公寓相关的储物柜以及是否拥有或租用。
Low-EffLow-Efficiency (furnace)低效的
LRLiving Room also L/R 客厅LR VS Fam Rm 属于公域的客厅，通常是玄关入门后最重要的空间，让人可在此停留，再连结通往餐厅等其他区域。
LuxLuxury ( as in Lux condo)豪华的
LvlLevel 层
LVRLoan-to-Value Ratio贷款对资产值比例
YesMaint（aka Condo Fees）this refers to the monthly maintenance fee(这是指每月维护费用)
MicroMicrowave 微波炉
MLS多重服务上市系统Multiple listing service URL
MLS#MLS number for internal use or a quick reference.内部使用的MLS编号
MnMain (as in Mn Floor or MnFl or MnFlr or M/F) 一般指一楼
MnthsMonths, also Mos or Mth 月
MsrmtsMeasurements 测量数据
NrNear 靠近
OccupThis will tell you if the unit is occupied by the owner or a tenant（占用：这将告诉您该单元是否由业主或租户占用）Occupied or Occupancy
O/LooksOverlooks (as in dining room O/looks Living Room or DR O/Looks LR) 能一眼看过去，俯瞰
ONHWPOntario New Home Warranty Program
YesPark/Drivewhere is the parking space指示与物业相关的停车位置
YesPark Typeis the parking space owned or rented?
YesPark Spcs# of parking spaces
YesPark /Moextra monthly fee for parking
Prk Lev/UnitWill indicate the level and parking space number.指出停车楼层和车位
PartPartly (as in Part finished basement) 一般指部分装修的地下室
PCPiece , also pc (as in 3PC bathroom, 3pc BR) 件
Pets PermThis refers to whether or not pets are permitted in the building – most condos have restrictions based on number of pets and size
PITIprincipal, interest, taxes, and insurance with regard to a mortgage or loan.本金 利息 税收 保险
PINthis is a number that the city uses to identify the property
PossessionThis is the Seller’s ideal closing date
YesPrk Lgl Dscthis is the parking legal description (which is not usually the same number as the real parking spot)这是停车法律说明（通常与实际停车位数量不同）
YesPrkg Inclis the maintenance fee for the parking spot included in the maintenance fee?维修费中包含的停车位维修费是多少？
Pk spot #车位编号
YesProp FeaturesLists amenities that are nearby.列出附近的设施
PwdrPowder (as in Powdr Room, Pwdr Rm)洗手间
REAReal Estate Agent or Realtor房产经纪
RecRecreation (as in Rec Room or Rec Rm)
ResResidence, also residential (as in zoned Res)居住的
Retirementis the building a dedicated retirement community?
R/IRough-In (as in R/I fireplace or dishwasher)预留管道
RmRoom房间
R/Oreverse osmosis (water purification system)一种净水系统
RreaRegistered Real Estate Agent注册房产经纪
SdsSides (as in Sides on the park)在…边上
SecSecurity安全
SepSeperate (as in Sep Living Room and Dining Room or Sep LR/DR)分开的或分门
SgwoSliding Glass Door Walkout玻璃推拉门
SlSliding (as in Sl glass patio doors)推拉门
SolSolarium 太阳能的
SpecSpecial特殊的
Spec Desigthis refers to special designation of the building, for example, if it’s a heritage building
SPISSeller Property Information Statement卖家签的关于房屋的一份文件，会把他们知道的关于房屋的信息写在上面。这是指卖方物业信息声明，这是一些卖家完成的文件，他们披露了他们对物业的了解 - Y表示SPIS可用，N表示不是（通常是公寓的N）
SqSquare (as in 100 sq feet)
SqftSquare Feet平方尺 $1m^2=10.76 ft^2$
Status CertThis refers to whether or not a status certificate for the building has already been ordered – your realtor will care about this but you don’t have to
StvStove 烤箱
SysSystem ( as in Security Sys or Sec Sys)系统
YesTaxes inclare taxes included in the maintenance fee? (usually no)
Title物业所有权
TbaTo Be Announced or Arranged 等通知安排
YesTot Prk SpcsTotal number of parking spaces停车位总数
TTCToronto Transit Commission (bus and subway system in Toronto)多伦多地铁和车站
TLCTender Loving Care meaning needs some repairs. 修缮
UFFIUrea Formaldihide Foam Insulation尿素甲醛绝缘材料,这通常是空白的，但如果建筑物中有已知的UFFI，可能会在这里公开
UpgrUpgraded (as in carpet upgr or upgr cabinets) 升级了的
WWith (as in comes w all appliances)
Washroomsnumber of washrooms – 1×4 means a 4 piece bath with tub with shower, 1×3 means a 3 piece bath with tub or shower, 1×2 is a powder room with no bath or shower
YesWater inclare water charges included in the maintenance fee? (usually yes)
WinWindow (as in Win covering or Win Cov)窗
W/IWalk-In (as in W/I closet) 走入式衣橱
W/LWhere Laid ( as in carpet W/L) also W/Laid
W/OWalk-Out (as in Basement Apartment W/O or W/O to deck)走出式地下室
2To (as in a short walk 2 the park) 去..
4+2The number after the + means in the basement. Example: Bedrooms: 4+2 imeans 4 BR on main or upper floors and 2 in basement.
10rated high as in 10 out of 10
